Thames river anglers association campaign

We had the great opportunity to work with a well-known anglers association in London, Ontario. We have been asked to create a new user-friendly and responsive website and lunch the promotional campaign.

Development Section

website design and development

We created a simple and understandable an easy web site to use and communicate with. It is loading fast, has a high level of security and adaptive for any screen size. We did a huge research work and user experience testing to figure out what would be the best approach for the TRAA.

PRINTED ADVERTISING AND CLOTHING

As the TRAA is a volunteer the organization we faced with an issue of how to monetize the brand and make it recognizable. We have done a lot of print advertising, invitations and posters, as well as branded clothing and office stuff to support the campaign.
